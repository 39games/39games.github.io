About Me

My name is Darko Stamenkovic — a Unity developer and music producer with a strong focus on shipping complete, polished projects. I specialize in gameplay-driven systems, performance-friendly architecture, and clean, maintainable code.

Over the years, I’ve published multiple games on Google Play and itch.io, handling everything from core gameplay mechanics and UI systems to audio, optimization, deployment, and post-launch fixes. My work emphasizes clarity, responsiveness, and player feel — systems should not only work, but feel right.

Alongside game development, I run 39 Beats, a music project focused on dark, atmospheric hip-hop production. Music plays a large role in how I approach games, influencing pacing, mood, and moment-to-moment experience.

I’ve worked as a freelancer on platforms like Fiverr and Upwork, collaborating with clients worldwide on prototypes, full releases, and system refactors. Whether working solo or with a team, my goal is always the same: build reliable systems, ship real products, and keep improving with every release.
